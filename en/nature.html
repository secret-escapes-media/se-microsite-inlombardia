You don’t have to search for Lombardy’s natural beauty - it’s right there in front of you.

Lombardy is actually one of the greenest parts of Italy. There are 25 natural parks for you to explore. Some of the mountains in the Bergamo Alps reach 3000 metres above sea level and you’ll find an array of Alpine wildlife in Stelvio National Park, including ibexes and chamois. For a little bit of everything, Brescia’s perfect mix of mountains and coastline is showcased in Alto Garda. Get on your bike, put on your skates, grab your hiking boots. There’s a beautiful adventure to be had and every inch of it is Instagram-worthy.
