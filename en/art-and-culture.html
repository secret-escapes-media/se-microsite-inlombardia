Explore a world of creativity and inspiration.

Italy is synonymous with world-class art. It’s a cultural melting pot and Lombardy is the ideal region to soak it all in. Whether you’re after design or history, or a little of both, there’s culture to be found all over Lombardy. Don’t just take our word for it - Mantua is Italy’s Capital of Culture and the entire city has been declared as one of 10 UNESCO World Heritage Sites in the region. Want to see it all? Start with Varese’s sacred mountains and rock carvings, then take a trip to the historic settlement of Crespi d'Adda before awing at one of the most famous paintings in the world: Da Vinci’s Last Supper, which hangs in Milan.
