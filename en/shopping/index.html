“Fashion is instant language.” Miuccia Prada.

There is no place in the world more stylish than Milan. It’s the home of renowned fashion powerhouses Prada, Armani and Versace. You don’t need to wait until September’s Fashion Week for sartorial inspiration, it’s here all year round. Lombardy’s shopping charms don’t begin and end with designer fashion so you’ll definitely need to save some space in your luggage. It’s also a fabulous place to purchase artisan crafts. Music lovers will be swept away by the master violin makers in Cremona and if fine footwear is your thing, step on over to the town of Vigevano to pick up the finest pair of shoes handmade in Italian leather.
