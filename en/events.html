There’s one thing for certain: you’ll never be bored in Lombardy.

Milan Fashion Week is only the start of the annual events and celebrations in Lombardy. A holiday in the region isn’t just about lounging by a gorgeous lake (although don’t let us stop you), it’s the ideal destination for holiday-goers who can’t sit still for even a second. Sports fans arrive in their thousands for the Monza Grand Prix and design lovers can’t resist a purchase or two at the world-famous furniture fair. You’ll be spoilt for choice.
